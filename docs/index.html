    • Reddit IPO Equity
    Research Report

    Overview: Authored a 19-page equity research report analyzing Reddit’s IPO valuation. Conducted financial analysis using Excel and R to benchmark Reddit against industry peers such as Meta and Snap. The report included revenue breakdowns, historical trend analysis, and forward-looking projections.

    Analysis Details:

    • Collected and analyzed Reddit’s financial data from 10-K filings
    • Created data visualizations and scenario-based valuation models
    • Validated assumptions with independent market research to align with industry trends

    Valuation Metrics & Ratios Used (applied to FCFE model):

    • Price-to-Earnings (P/E)
    • Price-to-Book (P/B)
    • Return on Equity (ROE)
    • Return on Assets (ROA)

    Results:

    • Free Cash Flow to the Firm (FCFF) was negative in nearly every year of Reddit’s operating history, prompting a shift to alternative valuation methods for the IPO recommendation.
    • Projected yearly revenue follows a logistic growth curve with a maximum value of approximately $43.5 billion around FY 2040 (source: Desmos).
    • Recommendation: Investors may consider buying at an appropriate entry point but should be cautious of long-term stagnation, as growth is expected to plateau by 2040.

    Spam or Email

    Overview: Developed a logistic regression-based email classifier to distinguish spam from legitimate emails using natural language processing (NLP) techniques. The project focused on accurate spam detection through text preprocessing, feature engineering, and model optimization.

    Key Features Engineered:

    • Character and word counts
    • Punctuation and symbol usage
    • HTML tag detection and structural patterns

    Models Used:

    • Logistic Regression
    • Linear Regression (for exploratory analysis)
    • Natural Language Processing techniques

    Results:

    • Achieved 91% accuracy in spam classification using GridSearchCV to fine-tune model hyperparameters
    • Used precision, recall, and F1-score to evaluate model robustness across different email types
    • Identified optimization opportunities for improving classification reliability across varied datasets
